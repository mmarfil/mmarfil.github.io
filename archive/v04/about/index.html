Hello. I’m Marcelo Marfil, a 26 year old visual designer and newlywed husband. I live in Sao Paulo, Brazil with my lovely wife and our little cat Alfredo.

In the past, I’ve worked with great companies including Acrylic Software, AppZapper, BakedCode, Droplr, Embraceware, Metaclassy, Strobe, Taptivate and Vemedio.

When not at work, you can find me obsessively reading books, feeding my addiction of strawberry frappuccinos or spending quality time with my loved ones.

By the way: is there something I can do for you? I design icons and user interfaces for iOS, Mac and web apps. If you’re interested in working with me, please send me an email. I'd love to hear from you.

Get in touch. You can also find me on Twitter or Google+.
